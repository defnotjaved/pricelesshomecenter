Our Story

27 Years of Making
Homes Priceless

Since 1999, Priceless Home Center has been the heartbeat of shopping in Trincity. We started with a simple mission: bring top-quality home goods to every family at prices they could actually afford, and that mission has never changed.

Today, with two thriving locations inside East Gates Mall and Trincity Mall, we're still that same beloved store, just bigger, better, and more popular than ever. Thousands of families across Trinidad trust us for everything from kitchen appliances to home decor.
